# AI & Generated Content Guidelines

We use AI (Antigravity, Claude, Grok, etc.) to help scale content, but everything must stay on-brand.

## Rules
- Always review and edit AI output to match Pixel Journey voice
- Never post raw AI text without human touch
- Keep the friendly, educational, slightly playful tone
- Include verifiable on-chain facts when relevant
- Use the post templates from `post-database/` as style anchors

## Good Practice
- Use the prompts in `prompts/` folder as starting points
- Run generated threads through the Px Content Studio for graphic pairing
- Tag posts appropriately so they can be reused later

The goal is **human creativity amplified by AI**, not AI-generated spam.
