# Social Media & Community Content Standards

## Posting Cadence
- Aim for 1–3 posts per day across the 6 pillars
- Rotate pillars to avoid fatigue
- Use the Px Content Studio + daily suggestions for consistency

## Thread Best Practices
- Start with a strong hook
- Keep individual tweets under 280 characters when possible
- End with a clear CTA + relevant hashtags
- Include verifiable on-chain mechanics when educational

## Community Interaction
- Always engage with replies from WAXFAMs
- Celebrate holder activity and Pixal showcases
- Never be overly salesy or hype-driven

## Hashtag Strategy
Core: #PixelJourney #WAX #Pixal
Situational: #HotOrNot #WAXFAMs #Verifiable

Use 2–5 hashtags maximum per post.
