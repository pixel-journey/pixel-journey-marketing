# Brand Voice & Messaging Standards

**Core Voice**: Friendly, educational, forward-thinking, retro-pixel enthusiastic.

**Key Phrases**:
- "pixel-perfect"
- "on-chain verifiable"
- "trustless & fun"
- "WAXFAMs & Pixel Journey"

**Emoji Palette**: 🛸 🪅 🕹️ 🎮 (use 2-4 per post, never spammy).

**Never**:
- Overly corporate language
- Financial advice tone
- FOMO or hype without substance

Always tie back to Explore / Learn / Enjoy Web3.
